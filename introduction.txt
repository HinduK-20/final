MSRIT 
Ramaiah Institute of Technology, formerly known as M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology, is an autonomous private engineering college located in Bangalore in the Indian state of Karnataka. 

MCA DEPARTMENT
The Department of Master of Computer Applications was established in 1997 with the objective of producing high quality professionals to meet the demands of the emerging field of Computer Applications. The Department of Master of Computer Applications is recognized as a Research Center under Visvesvaraya Technological University in 2012.
